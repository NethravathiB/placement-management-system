About placement management system

Placement Management System manages student information in the college with regard to placement. It improves existing system . It has the facility of maintaining the details of the student, thereby reducing the manual work. It will save time and energy which are spending in making reports and collecting data.

  • The “placement management software” or system helps the students, company to register and communicate all the information in the portal.
  • The users can easily get access to the portal and also the data can be retrieved easily within no time..
  • The placement process is the process by which a recruiter helps a client fill one of its open positions through the successful matching of the job's requirements to what a potential candidate offers in the form of skills
